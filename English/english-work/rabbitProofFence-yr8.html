INTRODUCTION

The movie: Rabbit-Proof Fence examines belonging and identity in the First Nations People through the experiences of the Stolen Generation. it follows the story of three girls from said Stolen Generation torn away from their family. The main character Molly escapes Moore River with her sister, Daisy and cousin, Gracie, to return home to her family.

PART 1

The Stolen Generation is First Nations People that were forcibly taken away from their parents, homes and lands. The Stolen Generation conscisted of all children usually from the ages of 16 to 21 years old of age (or sometimes younger).

PART 2

The people of the Stolen Generation/First Nations People had and still have quite, an important connection to the land in the many ways they treat it and use it. In one of the first scenes of the movie, they refer to things such as The Spirit Bird. which shows beliefs and other prospectives.
